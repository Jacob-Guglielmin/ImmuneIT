About

ImmuneIT is a web application designed to help healthcare employees learn about cybersecurity. The application is designed to be used by both employees and managers.


Employees have access to ImmuneIT's company-tailored cybersecurity quiz, as well as a visualization of their progress to date in their cybersecurity training.


Managers are able to track their employees' progress through the cybersecurity training, enter new, company-specific questions into the cybersecurity question database, and generate simulated phishing emails using ImmuneIT's AI integration.
